#dm-lore

The full truth of the Green Silence should remain uncertain for now.

It may not have had a single cause. It may have been part natural disaster, part magical containment, part elven ritual, and part deliberate erasure. Different accounts may all be partly correct.

What matters at this stage is that the Green Silence was not simply “the forest growing larger.” It was a continental trauma. It changed borders, erased settlements, buried roads, and made the south-western forests into one of the most culturally and magically important regions of Arkhaven.

The elves know more than they say, but even they may not know everything.

Some of their oldest records may be missing. Some may have been sealed. Some may be alive in ways that make reading them dangerous.

The Silence may have been caused by the forest defending itself.

It may have been caused by the elves defending the world.

It may have been caused by something beneath the roots waking up.

Or it may have been caused by the need to bury a truth that could not be allowed to survive in the open.

For now, the truth should stay layered, contradictory, and uncomfortable.
