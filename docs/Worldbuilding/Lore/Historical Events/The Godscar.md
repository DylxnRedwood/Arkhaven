# The Godscar

The **Godscar** is a vast continental fracture system running through large portions of [Arkhaven](../../Locations/Continents/Arkhaven.md).

It is regarded as one of the defining geographic and historical features of the continent.

The Godscar consists of enormous vertical crevasses, cliff systems, broken plateaus, deep lakes, river channels, unstable rock formations, and exposed geological strata extending across multiple regions.

## Overview

The exact origin of the Godscar remains disputed.

Most religious, historical, and geological traditions agree that the formation of the scar was catastrophic in scale.

The event reshaped:

- Rivers
- Mountain systems
- Trade routes
- Settlements
- Political borders
- Religious interpretation

Large sections of the continent were permanently altered.

## Geography

The Godscar varies considerably in width and depth.

Some regions consist of narrow canyon systems.

Others contain:

- Vast cliffs
- Deep lakes
- Broken terrain
- Flooded caverns
- Unstable rock shelves
- Vertical waterfalls
- Isolated plateaus

Travel across the scar is difficult and often dangerous.

## Veinstone Deposits

The Godscar contains the largest known concentrations of [Veinstone](../../Materials/Veinstone.md) in Lazuril.

Exposed mineral veins are visible in several cliff faces and lower rock formations throughout the region.

Some deposits appear as dark crystalline growths embedded directly within the fractured stone.

The presence of Veinstone has contributed significantly to mining interest, religious attention, and political conflict surrounding the scar.

## The Red Lakes

Several lakes and waterways connected to the Godscar display unusual red coloration.

This effect is widely believed to be associated with Veinstone-rich regions beneath or surrounding the water.

The colour varies between locations and environmental conditions.

Consumption of water taken directly from heavily affected lakes is generally discouraged.

Historical reports describe:

- Severe illness
- Hallucinations
- Fever
- Violent dreams
- Behavioural instability
- Long-term sickness

The exact cause of these effects remains uncertain.

## Settlement

Despite the dangers, several settlements exist near the Godscar.

Communities are typically associated with:

- Mining
- Trade crossings
- Pilgrimage routes
- Relic recovery
- Research expeditions
- Religious observation

Some settlements are temporary or highly isolated.

## Religious Importance

The Godscar holds major religious significance across Arkhaven.

Different faiths interpret the scar differently.

Some regard it as evidence of divine punishment or conflict.

Others interpret it as:

- A sacred wound
- A site of revelation
- A boundary between ages
- A scar left by divine intervention

No single interpretation is universally accepted.

## Exploration

The Godscar attracts:

- Scholars
- Miners
- Pilgrims
- Relic hunters
- Churches
- Archive expeditions
- Adventurers

Large areas remain poorly mapped.

Certain lower regions are inaccessible without specialised equipment or climbing infrastructure.

## Current Status

The Godscar remains one of the most politically, economically, and religiously important regions in Arkhaven.

Continued discovery of Veinstone deposits and ancient structures within the scar has increased institutional interest in the region.

At the same time, many areas remain unstable, dangerous, or unexplored.
