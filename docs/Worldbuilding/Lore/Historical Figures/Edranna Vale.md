# Edranna Vale

**Edranna Vale** was a disputed oracle associated with the [War of the False Saints](../../Arkhaven%20Events/War%20of%20the%20False%20Saints.md).

## Status

Disputed.

## Associated Tradition

Visions, warning, and prophecy.

## Historical Role

Edranna was a rural oracle whose warnings reportedly prevented several settlements from being destroyed during the unrest.

Her supporters treated her as a saint after her death.

## Later Dispute

Some prophecies attributed to her appear to have been added later. The original accounts suggest she may have been a genuine seer or divinely inspired figure, but her posthumous cult altered much of the record.

Modern Scriptors usually classify her as a Captured Saint.
