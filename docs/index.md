# Lazuril

Welcome to the Lazuril Wiki.

This wiki serves as the primary worldbuilding archive for **Lazuril**, a homebrew Dungeons & Dragons 5.5e campaign setting designed by [DylxnRedwood](https://github.com/DylxnRedwood).

The setting currently focuses on the continent of Arkhaven, a land shaped by divine catastrophe, religious conflict, fractured history, ancient forests, and long-standing political tension.

The wiki is intended to function both as:

- A living worldbuilding reference
- An Obsidian-compatible lore archive
- A campaign setting repository
- A historical and geographical reference for players and DMs

## Core Historical Events

Several major events define the known history of Lazuril:

- [The Godscar](Worldbuilding/Lore/Historical%20Events/The%20Godscar.md)
- [The Green Silence](Worldbuilding/Lore/Historical%20Events/The%20Green%20Silence.md)
- [War of the False Saints](Worldbuilding/Lore/Historical%20Events/War%20of%20the%20False%20Saints.md)

These events continue to influence religion, politics, geography, scholarship, and cultural identity throughout Arkhaven.

## The World of Lazuril

Lazuril is a fantasy world developed for long-form storytelling, campaign play, historical continuity, and interconnected lore.

The setting includes:

- Multiple continents
- Major religions and church structures
- Long-lived elven archive cultures
- Divine and political conflict
- Ancient ruins and lost histories
- Distinct biomes and regional cultures
- Institutional magic traditions
- Large-scale historical timelines

The world is designed to support both grounded political storytelling and high fantasy adventures.
